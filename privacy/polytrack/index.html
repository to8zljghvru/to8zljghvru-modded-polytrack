PolyTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

1. Introduction

Welcome to PolyTrack! This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected while playing the game. By playing PolyTrack, players agree to the collection and use of information as described here.

2. Information Collected

When playing PolyTrack, the following types of information may be collected:

  • IP Addresses: The game servers log players' IP addresses for security and operational purposes. These IP addresses are kept for a maximum of 30 days. In some cases, they may be retained longer if necessary to enforce IP blocking or other security measures.
  • Log Timestamps: Timestamps of player interactions with the game servers are logged for troubleshooting and security purposes. These timestamps are linked to a specific IP address but are not associated with any User ID or any other personal information. These timestamps are retained for a maximum of 30 days.
  • User ID: A unique, randomly generated identifier is assigned to each player to differentiate between users when communicating with the game servers. The nickname, country selection and leaderboard timestamps are associated with this User ID.
  • Nicknames: Players can choose to set a non-unique nickname. This nickname is optional and will be shown on the public in-game leaderboard. If a player is logged in to a CrazyGames or Poki account on that platform's website, the nickname may be set automatically to the account username, but the player can change or remove it at any time.
  • Country: Players can optionally select their country from a predefined list. This information is used to display country flags on the public in-game leaderboard.
  • Leaderboard Timestamps: When a player record is added to the public in-game leaderboard, a timestamp of when the record was achieved is stored and may be displayed on the leaderboard. This timestamp is associated with the User ID.

3. How Information is Used

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To maintain the security of the game.
  • To troubleshoot and resolve server issues that may occur.
  • To differentiate between users when they interact with the game servers.
  • To display nicknames, country flags and timestamps on the public in-game leaderboard.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

IP addresses are processed based on legitimate interests in maintaining the security of the game and addressing any server-related issues. IP addresses are not linked to specific User IDs.

Log Timestamps are processed based on legitimate interests in troubleshooting and ensuring the proper functioning of the game servers. These timestamps are not linked to specific User IDs.

User IDs are necessary for the operation of the in-game leaderboard and to differentiate between players. This processing is essential for providing the leaderboard functionality.

Nicknames and country selections are processed based on players' consent, as they voluntarily provide this information to enhance their gaming experience.

Leaderboard Timestamps are processed based on legitimate interests in providing a complete leaderboard experience, allowing players to see when records were achieved.

5. Data Retention

IP address logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days. If an IP address is flagged for malicious activity, it may be kept longer to prevent further issues.

Log timestamps are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

User IDs are retained for as long as necessary to maintain leaderboard functionality and historical records.

Nicknames and country selections can be changed or removed by players at any time through the game interface.

Leaderboard Timestamps are retained for as long as necessary to maintain leaderboard functionality and historical records.

6. Third-Party Services

The game servers are hosted on Hostinger.com. While personal information is not shared with third parties, Hostinger may have access to server logs, including IP addresses. Please review Hostinger's privacy policy for more details on their data handling practices.

If you play PolyTrack on websites other than Kodub.com, those websites may also collect or process data under their own privacy policies. Please review the applicable policies below:

  • CrazyGames
  • Poki
  • MSN Games
  • itch.io

7. Local Storage

PolyTrack uses the web browser's local storage to save game data and settings. This data can be cleared by the player at any time through their browser settings, but some information may still remain on the game servers as described above.

Local storage data for the nickname, country selection, car designs and replays are synced to the game servers to enable access across different devices and for leaderboard functionality. This synced data is associated with the User ID.

8. Multiplayer

When playing multiplayer, players may connect directly through peer-to-peer connections using WebRTC technology. This means that players' IP addresses may be visible to the host of the multiplayer session. Players should be aware of this when joining multiplayer games.

Nicknames and country selections are also visible to other players during multiplayer sessions.

9. User Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), players have rights regarding their personal data, including the right to access, rectify, and erase data. For any inquiries about these rights, please reach out using the contact information below.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

When this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here and the "Last updated" date will be updated accordingly. The revised policy will take effect 14 days after posting; during that 14-day period, the previous policy will continue to apply. Continuing to access PolyTrack after the 14-day period indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy, please get in touch at info@kodub.com or any of the other contact methods listed on the contact page at https://www.kodub.com/contact.
